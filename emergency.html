Emergency Support

If you're feeling overwhelmed, try the following techniques and reach out if needed — no login or data required.

Grounding Technique (5-4-3-2-1)

This method helps calm anxiety by anchoring you to your surroundings.

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste
